### User Personas

## Introduction

User personas represent the primary users of the NourishBridge platform. They help understand user goals, challenges, and expectations, ensuring the system is designed around real-world needs.

---

# Persona 1: Food Donor

**Name:** Rahul Sharma

**Age:** 34

**Occupation:** Event Organizer

### Background

Rahul frequently organizes weddings and corporate events where surplus food remains after the event.

### Goals

* Donate surplus food quickly.
* Find nearby verified NGOs.
* Ensure food reaches people in need.
* Track the donation status.

### Pain Points

* Doesn't know which NGO to contact.
* Manual communication takes too much time.
* Lack of trust and transparency.

### Technical Skills

Basic to Intermediate

### Features Used

* Registration & Login
* Create Donation
* Upload Food Images
* View Nearby NGOs
* Track Donations
* Donation History
